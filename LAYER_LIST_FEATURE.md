# ✅ Layer List Added for Easy Access

I have implemented a **Layer List** to make it easy to see and access all elements, even those hidden behind groups.

## How it Works
1. **Easy Selection:** Standard "Click to select" behavior is active (easy to move groups).
2. **Accessing Hidden Items:**
   - If you can't click an item because a group is blocking it:
   - **Click on empty space** to deselect everything.
   - Look at the panel on the right.
   - You will see a **"Layers"** list showing all your objects.
   - Click any item in the list (e.g., "Subtitle" or "Logo") to **select it instantly!** 🎯

## Why this is better
- You get the "Easy Moving" of groups (Selection Box).
- You get "Total Access" to hidden layers via the List.
- No need to guess where to click!

## Verify
1. Reload.
2. Deselect everything.
3. Check the right sidebar → You should see a list of layers.
4. Click a layer in the list → It gets selected on the canvas! ✅
